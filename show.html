Flower Show 2024

Join us for the annual Flower Show at Lal Bagh Botanical Garden, taking place from February 15 to February 25, 2024. This vibrant event showcases a stunning array of flowers and plants from around the world.

The theme for this year's show is "Colors of Nature," highlighting the beauty and diversity of floral species. Visitors can expect to see breathtaking floral displays, including exotic blooms, traditional Indian flowers, and innovative garden designs.

Throughout the event, there will be various activities, including workshops on gardening, floral arrangement demonstrations, and guided tours of the garden. It's a perfect opportunity for gardening enthusiasts and families to learn and enjoy nature.

Special attractions this year include a children's corner with fun activities, a photography contest, and food stalls offering delicious local cuisine. Don't miss the chance to capture the beauty of the flowers and create lasting memories.

Entry tickets are available at the garden entrance and online. Early bird discounts are offered for tickets purchased before January 31, 2024. Make sure to mark your calendars and be part of this floral extravaganza!
