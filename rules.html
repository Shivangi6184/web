Rules of Lal Bagh Botanical Garden

1. Entry Fee: Visitors must pay the entry fee at the entrance. Children under a certain age may enter for free.

2. Opening Hours: The garden is open from 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Please respect these hours.

3. No Littering: Please dispose of waste in designated bins. Littering is strictly prohibited.

4. No Smoking: Smoking is not allowed within the garden premises to maintain a clean and healthy environment.

5. Respect Nature: Do not pick flowers, damage plants, or disturb wildlife. Help us preserve the beauty of the garden.

6.Photography: Photography is allowed, but please do not use tripods or professional equipment without prior permission.

7. Pets: Pets are not allowed in the garden to ensure the safety and comfort of all visitors.

8. Stay on Paths: Please stick to designated paths and areas to protect the flora and fauna.

9.Group Visits: For large groups, prior permission may be required. Please contact the management for arrangements.

10. Follow Guidelines: Always follow the instructions of the garden staff and any posted signs for a safe and enjoyable visit.
